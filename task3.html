Biography

Narendra Damodardas Modi, born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, India, is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India. He has been a key figure in Indian politics, known for his leadership and policies.

Political Journey

Narendra Modi began his political career as a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He served in various capacities within the party, eventually becoming the Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2001.

Prime Minister of India

Narendra Modi assumed office as the Prime Minister of India on May 26, 2014, after leading the BJP to a decisive victory in the general elections. His tenure has been marked by several initiatives, including Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Make in India, and demonetization.

Personal Life

Narendra Modi is known for his disciplined lifestyle and strong work ethic. He is a vegetarian and has a keen interest in yoga. Modi is also active on social media platforms, using them as a means to connect with the public.

Awards and Recognitions

Narendra Modi has received several awards and honors for his contributions to Indian politics. In 2020, he was named Time magazine's Person of the Year. His leadership style and policies continue to shape India's political landscape.
